FLEXIBLE BODY

A cat's flexible body is a marvel of nature, allowing them to twist, turn, and contort with remarkable agility. Their spine is exceptionally flexible, featuring an incredible range of motion that aids in hunting, climbing, and navigating various terrains. This adaptability is due to their unique skeletal structure and highly elastic muscles, enabling them to squeeze through tight spaces and land gracefully from great heights.
